About Us

Pacific Real Estate Buyers is a team of professionals who facilitate the purchase of Californian homes from sellers who wish to sell quickly and for cash as an alternative to the time and costs of using a real estate agency. There are many options for how the property can be purchased, and we will find the right one for your needs. Let us show you how we can help you today!

Process

At Pacific Real Estate Buyers, we have a very simpe process that allows you to sell you home quickly!

  • Conversation

    We have a conversation about your goals and arrange to see the property.

  • Establish Price

    Together we determine a fair price and extend an offer to you. There are no commission or real estate agent fees!

  • Legal

    We sign legal contracts with an Escrow or Title Company to protect both parties. We can close in days, not months. Average time to close is 20 days or less.

  • Move

    You can move free and clear of any worries! You’ve saved on months of listing, commissions and fees and now have cash in your hands to move on with your life.
